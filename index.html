Work

I am an award-winning journalist relocating to London after four years at the Sydney Morning Herald. I have covered major health and science stories,live blogged conflict and elections, and broken investigations that have sparked government reviews and regulatory changes.

Cybersecurity
Sensitive NSW medical records at risk of falling into hackers' hands
I was leaked a copy of an internal cybersecurity audit which found NSW hospitals were failing to meet basic cybersecurity standards despite measures costing taxpayers $40 million a year.
Jun 2025

Investigative
In the shadow of a mining giant, children crawl in poison
I travelled with photographer Rhett Wyman to the NSW Far West to investigate the disproportionate effect of lead poisoning on Indigenous children. This investigation was part of a body of work for which I was named 2025 Kennedy Young Journalist of the Year.
Sep 2024
→
Pharmaceuticals
The people unknowingly poisoning themselves with common vitamins
I turned a reader tip-off and a previously unreported journal paper into a multiplatform investigation on vitamin B6 toxicity. My reporting over 18 months helped spark regulatory changes which saw common vitamins removed from the shelves. .
Feb 2024
→
Interview
Anthony Fauci on Australia's COVID response, AI and the next pandemic
